Yeelo Community

Community Guidelines & EULA

Last updated: June 11, 2026 · Contact: help@yeelo.cloud

These guidelines explain how users should behave in Yeelo’s formalwear inspiration, AI try-on, messaging, and community features.

Use Yeelo respectfully, protect privacy, and only upload content you have permission to use.
01

Community Purpose

›

Yeelo is intended to help users explore bridal and formalwear styles, preview outfits with AI, share wedding or event inspiration, discuss looks, follow creators, and participate in a positive fashion-focused community. These guidelines apply to all uploads, AI try-on results, community posts, comments, messages, profile pages, reports, and interactions on Yeelo.

02

Respectful Conduct

›

Users must treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, threats, hateful conduct, stalking, malicious false reporting, or repeated hostile behavior are prohibited. Users may not attack or degrade people based on protected characteristics.

03

Photo Consent and Privacy

›

Do not upload another person’s photo, body image, private fitting image, wedding photo, or profile image without permission. This includes friends, clients, models, public figures, minors, or people appearing in group photos.

You may not share another person’s private information, including addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, private messages, IDs, payment details, wedding details, boutique records, or confidential customer information.

04

AI Try-On Rules

›

Users may not use AI try-on features to create harmful, deceptive, harassing, sexual, exploitative, illegal, impersonating, or privacy-invasive content. Users may not attempt to bypass safety systems, manipulate AI behavior, generate misleading identity-related content, or use AI outputs to defame or deceive others.

05

Fashion and Fit Disclaimer

›

Yeelo content is for digital visualization and inspiration. AI try-on results do not confirm product fit, garment quality, tailoring requirements, fabric behavior, availability, delivery timing, or event suitability. Users should consult qualified boutiques, tailors, designers, or stylists for real-world decisions.

06

Prohibited Content

›
  • Illegal, harmful, violent, threatening, or exploitative content.
  • Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse.
  • Sexually explicit, pornographic, exploitative, or non-consensual intimate content.
  • Scams, phishing, impersonation, misleading promotions, or counterfeit claims.
  • Content that exposes another person’s private information.
  • Copyright-infringing photos, designer images, logos, product images, or artwork.
  • Spam, bots, fake engagement, scraping, or platform manipulation.
07

Intellectual Property

›

Users should only upload photos, dress images, logos, product references, lookbooks, wedding images, or creative materials that they own or are authorized to use. Unauthorized use of copyrighted photos, designer images, brand logos, boutique images, celebrity images, or third-party materials may result in removal or account enforcement.

08

Messaging and Style Interaction

›

Messaging and community interactions must remain respectful and style-focused. Users may not harass creators, pressure users, solicit illegal services, send spam, request sensitive personal data, or use chat features to abuse others.

09

Consumable Purchases and Fair Use

›

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with platform rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, manipulate item delivery, abuse refunds, or use purchased items to harass or manipulate others.

10

Reporting and Moderation

›

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, AI misuse, privacy violations, copyright infringement, impersonation, harassment, scams, misleading product claims, or other misuse. Yeelo may review reports using manual review, automated tools, safety signals, purchase records, moderation history, and community reports.

11

Enforcement

›

Yeelo may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, messaging restrictions, AI generation restrictions, purchase restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes.

✉

Contact

›

If you have questions about this document, AI try-on features, purchases, privacy, reports, or account matters, contact us at help@yeelo.cloud.

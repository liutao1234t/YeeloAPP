Yeelo Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026 · Contact: help@yeelo.cloud

This Privacy Policy explains how Yeelo protects information when users explore gowns, use AI try-on, and participate in the community.

Yeelo does not create biometric templates, bodyprints, faceprints, or identity databases from uploaded photos.
01

Information We Collect

›
  • Account information: username, email address, profile photo, bio, age or date of birth, style preferences, saved looks, and other information you choose to provide.
  • Uploaded photos: photos voluntarily submitted for AI dress try-on, profile images, community posts, or support requests.
  • AI try-on information: selected dress styles, uploaded references, generation settings, output history, feedback, and non-sensitive interaction signals.
  • Community and purchase data: posts, comments, messages, reports, likes, follows, saves, shares, consumable product identifiers, transaction identifiers, receipt validation data, delivery status, and support records.
02

Automatically Collected Information

›

When you use Yeelo, we may collect device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, IP address, diagnostics, crash logs, network information, and device identifiers where permitted. We may also collect usage data such as pages viewed, dress categories browsed, AI try-on activity, saved styles, community interactions, purchase interactions, and search activity.

03

Device Permissions

›

Yeelo may request access to camera and photos so you can upload images for dress try-on or community sharing. We may request notification permission for account, message, activity, generation, safety, or purchase notices. You can change permissions in device settings at any time.

04

AI Model and User Information Protection

›

Internally trained AI model

Yeelo uses internally managed AI models trained and optimized for dress try-on and fashion visualization. The model is designed to place gowns, formalwear, suits, veils, fabrics, silhouettes, and styling previews onto user-provided photos.

No identification use

Yeelo does not use uploaded photos or AI inputs for face recognition, body recognition, biometric identification, authentication, surveillance, law enforcement profiling, or sensitive attribute inference. We do not create biometric templates, faceprints, bodyprints, identity databases, or long-term biometric profiles from uploaded images.

Limited purpose

Uploaded photos are processed only to provide the requested dress try-on, display generated results, support content moderation, troubleshoot service errors, maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and operate the Service.

05

How We Use Information

›
  • To provide dress discovery, AI try-on, style recommendations, community features, messaging, and account functions.
  • To operate AI features by processing photos, selected outfits, generation settings, feedback, and safety signals.
  • To personalize content such as dresses, silhouettes, fabrics, community posts, and recommendations.
  • To process consumable purchases, validate receipts, deliver digital items, prevent fraud, and respond to support requests.
  • To detect harmful content, harassment, impersonation, privacy violations, AI misuse, fraud, spam, and unauthorized access.
06

Sharing of Information

›

We may share information with service providers that help operate Yeelo, including hosting, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting, customer support, payment verification, content moderation, security, fraud prevention, and infrastructure providers. We may share purchase-related information with app stores and payment platforms. We may disclose information when required by law or when needed to protect users, Yeelo, or the public.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense.

07

Consumable Purchase Data

›

Yeelo may offer consumable digital items. We may receive purchase-related information such as product identifier, transaction identifier, purchase status, receipt validation data, country, delivery status, and refund or support records. Consumable purchases are not auto-renewable subscriptions and do not automatically renew.

08

Data Retention

›

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. If you delete your account, we will take steps to delete or anonymize personal information, subject to reasonable exceptions for legal obligations, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, moderation records, security, backups, and compliance.

09

Your Rights and Choices

›

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request portability of certain personal information. You may update profile information in the app, revoke device permissions in device settings, or request account deletion by contacting help@yeelo.cloud.

10

Security and Children’s Privacy

›

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. The Service is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
